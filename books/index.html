Cownomics

The Holy Cow — Fulcrum of a Civilization · NotionPress, 2021

Cownomics sheds light on the need to move towards a sustainable, cruelty-free cow-based economy in India. The book brings together the lens of economics, ecology, and cultural heritage to make the case for a humane and productive relationship with bovine resources.
